Animals

If you have a goat, sheep,pig ,cow, horse or any animal you’d like to show or bring along feel free. You can always enjoy the  amusing animal parades from chickens to shires with an owl fly past, we even have ferret racing and we’ve seen rabbits, guineas, chicks, ducks, hedgehog and even tortoises.
